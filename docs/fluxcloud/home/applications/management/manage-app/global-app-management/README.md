# Global App Management

The **Global App Management** section provides centralized control over the full lifecycle of your deployed application across the FluxCloud.&#x20;

This is your operational hub for performing high-level actions across all node instances of your app. The tools here are especially useful for developers, sysadmins, and DevOps managing deployments or coordinating changes.

#### How To Access

> **Management → My Active Apps → Manage → Global App Management**

Open the **Management** tab in the **Applications** menu, select an application from **My Active Apps**, and click **Manage**.

***

### Overview of Tabs

#### 1. Control

The **Control** tab is your command center for managing the active state of your application.

This section empowers you to maintain uptime, manage upgrades, or troubleshoot critical issues—all from one place.

→ Read more about the [Control tab](broken-reference)

***

#### 2. Running Instances

The **Running Instances** tab gives you visibility into where and how your application is currently running across the FluxCloud.

→ Read more about [Running Instances](broken-reference)

***

#### 3. Cancel Subscription

The **Cancel Subscription** tab allows you to terminate your app’s presence on the FluxCloud

→ Read more about [Cancel Subscription](broken-reference)

***

### When to Use Global App Management

Use this section when you need:

* To manage the overall lifecycle of your app across the entire Flux network
* To perform high-level actions like redeploying, stopping, or removing your application
* A global view of where your app is running, including geographic distribution
* To cancel your app subscription or manage network-level availability
* To coordinate application changes that affect all instances, not just one node
