# 7. Confirm Settings

After SSH and notification settings are complete, you’ll land on a **final confirmation screen**.

***

### Confirmation Overview

Here, you can choose to:

* **Upload** your current config (notifications, SSH, blocked ports/repos) for reuse on future node installs
* **Download** the config as a `.json` or `.yaml` file for backup

<img src="/.gitbook/assets/Confirm Settings.png" alt=""/>

Click the **Apply** button to confirm everything.

Once applied:

* You’ll see a **“Configuration Complete”** screen
* You can now **close the configuration webpage**
