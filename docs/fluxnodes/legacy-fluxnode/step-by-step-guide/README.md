# Step-by-Step Guide

Running a FluxNode is a straightforward process once you understand the flow. This guide walks you through preparing your collateral, choosing your hosting environment, and installing the FluxNode software.

***

### Overview of Steps

You’ll complete three main stages:

1. **Prepare Flux Collateral** – Lock the required amount of FLUX for your chosen node tier while keeping full control of your funds.
2. **Choose Your Hosting Setup** – Decide between renting a VPS or running from home on your own hardware.
3. **Install the FluxNode Software** – Set up FluxOS, configure your node, and complete benchmarks to activate it.
