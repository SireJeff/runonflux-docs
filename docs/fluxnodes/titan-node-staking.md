# Titan Node Staking

Titan Nodes allow you to participate in supporting the Flux network with as little as **50 FLUX**.\
Your funds remain **non-custodial** and are managed via **Zelcore**, while the Flux team handles deployment on **enterprise-grade hardware** from partners like **Lumen Technologies** and **OVH Cloud**.

* **Security:** All transactions are signed and broadcast from a **multi-signature address**.
* **Rewards:** Earn native FLUX rewards (no Parallel Assets) with payouts twice a week.

This guide walks you through staking FLUX into Titan Nodes to earn rewards while strengthening the Flux decentralized infrastructure.

***

### Requirements

Before you begin, ensure you have:

* **Minimum Collateral:** 50 FLUX (main chain)
* **Maximum Collateral:** 40,000 FLUX total (10,000 FLUX per individual stake)
* **Zelcore Wallet** — Download [here](https://zelcore.io/)
* Stable internet connection and a supported browser (Chrome, Brave, or Edge)

> **Note:** d2FA (Decentralized Two-Factor Authentication) in Zelcore is recommended for added security.

***

### Step-by-Step Staking Process

#### 1. Access the Titan Dashboard

* Go to: [https://titan.runonflux.io/](https://titan.runonflux.io/)
* Click the login icon (top-right) and log in with your **ZelID** using:
  * Zelcore Desktop
  * QR code via Zelcore mobile, or
  * Manual signing inside Zelcore
* Accept the **Terms of Service** on first login.

<img src="/.gitbook/assets/Sign in.png" alt=""/>

#### 2. Start  Titan Node Setup

* Go to **Active Flux** tab → click **“+”** at the bottom-right to add a Titan Node.
* Select or Enter your FLUX amount (**50–10,000 per node**).
  * Please be mindful that a maximum limit of 40,000 FLUX applies per FluxID.
* Click **Next**.

<img src="/.gitbook/assets/Active Flux.png" alt=""/>

<img src="/.gitbook/assets/Activate Titan.png" alt=""/>

#### 3. Choose Lockup Duration

* Longer lockup = higher yield.
* During lockup, collateral cannot be withdrawn.
* Option: **Auto-reinvest** (collateral + rewards automatically re-lock after expiry).
  * This selection cannot be modified later on.

<img src="/.gitbook/assets/choose duration.png" alt=""/>

#### 4. Sign via Zelcore

* Click the **white fingerprint icon** to launch Zelcore and sign the request using your **d2FA PIN**.
* Alternatively, you can **manually sign the message** within Zelcore’s **ZelID application**.
* Once signing is complete, click **"Next"** to proceed.

<img src="/.gitbook/assets/Sign with zelcore.png" alt=""/>

#### 5. Register Titan Node

* Review details carefully.
* Sign using your Zelcore d2FA PIN or manual signing.
* Click **Register → Next**.

<img src="/.gitbook/assets/Register Titan.png" alt=""/>

#### 6. Send Funds to Titan

**Two options:**

* **Manual:** Send FLUX to the provided multi-sig address, including the correct MEMO.
* **Automated:** Click the fingerprint icon → approve transaction in Zelcore.

Once payment is made, return to Titan Dashboard → click **Finish**.

> **Important:** Double-check the wallet address and transaction memo before sending.

<img src="/.gitbook/assets/Send Funds.png" alt=""/>

#### 7. Wait for Confirmation

* Requires \~30 confirmations (\~60 min).
* Once confirmed, your Titan Node becomes **Active** and starts earning rewards.
* Congratulations, you have successfully become an integral participant in the FluxCloud network through Titan Nodes!

<img src="/.gitbook/assets/Finished.png" alt=""/>

### How to Redeem or Reinvest Your FLUX Rewards

Titan Nodes allow you to redeem rewards **before** your lock-up period ends, as long as you have **at least 50 FLUX** available for redemption. Rewards are distributed **twice per week**, every **Monday** and **Thursday** morning at around **9:00 AM UTC**.

All payouts are executed from Titan’s **multi-signature wallet** by the official Flux team, ensuring an additional layer of security.

You have **two options** for using your rewards:

1. **Redeem** — Send your FLUX rewards to a wallet address of your choice.
2. **Reinvest** — Use your rewards to create a new Titan Node, compounding your earnings.

<img src="/.gitbook/assets/Redeem and Reinvest.png" alt=""/>

***

### Redeeming Your FLUX Rewards

#### 1. Navigate to the Rewards Section

* On the Titan Dashboard, go to the **"Active Flux"** page.
* Locate your available rewards balance.
* Click the **"Redeem"** button next to your rewards.

<img src="/.gitbook/assets/Redeem1.png" alt=""/>

#### 2. Review the Redemption Summary

* You will see the **total amount available** for redemption.
* The displayed value will already have the **small redeem fee** deducted.
* Click **"Next"** to proceed.

#### 3. Select Destination Address

* Choose one of your existing FLUX addresses from the dropdown list.
* This is where your redeemed FLUX will be sent.
* Click **"Next"**.

<img src="/.gitbook/assets/Redeem2.png" alt=""/>

#### 4. Authenticate the Redemption

* **Option 1 (Recommended)**: Click the **ZelID icon** to automatically launch Zelcore and sign the request.
* **Option 2**: Manually sign the message in Zelcore via the **ZelID application**.

<img src="/.gitbook/assets/Redeem3.png" alt=""/>

#### 5. Submit the Request

* Once signed, click **"Submit"**.
* Your rewards will be transferred to the selected wallet during the next scheduled payout.

<img src="/.gitbook/assets/Redeem4.png" alt=""/>

***

### Reinvesting Your FLUX Rewards

Reinvesting allows you to use your rewards to **create a new Titan Node** without withdrawing them first.

#### 1. Navigate to Rewards and Click "Reinvest"

* From the **"Active Flux"** section, click the **"Reinvest"** button next to your rewards.

<img src="/.gitbook/assets/Reinvest1.png" alt=""/>

#### 2. Proceed Through Registration

* You’ll go through the **same Titan Node setup process** as when you first registered, **except for the initial amount entry step** (your reward amount is automatically filled).
* Steps you’ll see again:
  * **Select Lockup Period** — Longer lockups = higher APR.
  * **Sign via Zelcore**&#x20;
  * **Register Titan Node** — Review details before confirming.
  * **Confirm & Wait for Activation** — Around 60 minutes for 30 confirmations.

#### 3. Activation

* Once confirmed, your reinvested Titan Node will appear under **"Active"** in your dashboard.

***

### Reinvesting from an Expired Titan Node

If your **Titan Node lock-up period** has ended, it will move to the **"Expired"** section of the dashboard.

#### 1. Locate Your Expired Node

* At the **"Active Flux"** menu, click the **"Expired"** tab.

#### 2. Click "Reinvest"

* This will start the **registration process** using both:
  * Your original collateral
  * All accumulated rewards from that node

<img src="/.gitbook/assets/Expired Node.png" alt=""/>

#### 3. Complete the Setup

* Follow the same **Lockup → Sign → Register → Confirm** process.
* Your new Titan Node will appear in the **Active** section after \~60 minutes.

### Frequently Asked Questions

#### Do Titan stakers receive Parallel Asset snapshot rewards?

Yes — they are credited directly to your funding wallet, independent of Titan.

***

#### Do Titan stakers receive parallel asset mining rewards like traditional FluxNode operators?

No — Titan staking only provides native FLUX rewards at the displayed APR.
