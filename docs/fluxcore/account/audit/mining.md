# Mining

The Audit Mining tab provides a comprehensive history of your proof-of-work activities associated with your account. You will see a detailed data table with information about all your mining history.

* **Duration/Starting Time**: The date and time when your miner started, along with the duration of the mining session in hours.
* **Coin**: The cryptocurrency you mined.
* **Machine/CPUs/GPUs**: The name of the machine used for mining, its operating system, and the number of GPUs or CPUs.
* **Pool**: The mining pool to which you contributed, displayed as your saved name for it.
* **Est. Reward**: The estimated amount of rewards earned during the mining session, shown in the mined cryptocurrency.
* **Wallet**: The wallet to which the mined rewards were sent, displayed as your saved name for it.
* **Average Hashrate**: Your average hashrate over the entire mining duration.
* **Total Power Consumption**: The total power consumption during the mining session, measured in watts or kilowatts.

You also have tools to filter the data by specific assets and durations (e.g., 1 week, 1 month, 1 year) and a CSV download tool for easy export.
