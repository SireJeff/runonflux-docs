# Rewards Claimed

The Audit Rewards Claimed tab provides a comprehensive history of the rewards you have claimed for the machines you own on FluxEdge.

You will see a detailed data table with information about all your reward claims.

* **Date**: The date and time when you initiated the claim.
* **Type**: The type of claim, either Withdrawal or Transfer.
  * **Withdrawal**: A direct withdrawal of the rewards via the cryptocurrency FLUX to a specified wallet.
  * **Transfer**: A transfer of the rewards to your FluxEdge Balance, useful if you are both a provider and a customer and want to use the rewards to rent other machines.
* **Reward**: The amount you either transferred or withdrew.
* **Payment**: The coin and network for withdrawals, or FluxEdge for transfers.
* **Wallet**: The wallet address you initiated the withdrawal to, or FluxEdge for transfers.
* **Amount**: The equivalent amount of the reward in fiat currency.
* **Status**: The status of the claim. For successful withdrawals, you will receive a link to a block explorer. _Note: Withdrawals require manual intervention and can take up to 24 hours or longer._
