# Efficient Steps to Safely Cleanse Your Windows System of FluxCore

If you encounter issues with your machine while running FluxCore, it is sometimes advisable to perform a complete uninstallation of the application along with all related components. Here are the steps on how to do it on your Windows system.

1\. Two icons, “fluxcore” and “uninstall\_fluxcore,” should be visible on your desktop.

<img src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*pcTPoeKWZJKvsAZtqRW7LQ.png" alt=""/>

2\. Execute the “uninstall\_fluxcore” application to begin the uninstallation process.

<img src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*Zx6_Mzbdi0xRGNAlvRw-KA.png" alt=""/>

<img src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*0iq4CwpSN45a_chGqMsV6g.png" alt=""/>

3\. Following a successful uninstallation, navigate to the default location for all FluxCore-related files at C:\Users\XXX\Downloads. Delete all FluxCore-related files, including the fluxcore.exe download file and machine credentials (optional).

4\. As a final step, open your browser and clear its cookies to ensure that no FluxCore-related data is stored in them.
