# Claiming Rewards

### **Balance Claiming Tile**

This section shows your current unclaimed rewards and total rewards claimed from leasing machines on the marketplace. You can initiate a claim or view balance history.

<img src="/.gitbook/assets/image (9) (1).png" alt=""/>

#### **Claim Options**

1. **Withdraw:**
   * All claims are in FLUX and can be withdrawn to Mainnet, Ethereum, or Binance Smart Chain. Enter your wallet address, the amount, and click Claim. Fees and minimum withdrawal amounts vary by network. Note that withdrawals require manual intervention and can take up to 24 hours. Monitor the status through your Account Audit.
2. **Transfer:**
   * For providers who are also FluxEdge customers, transfer rewards to your FluxEdge balance for projects and deployments. Enter the USD amount and click Transfer Now. Ensure you use the same email for both FluxCore and FluxEdge.
