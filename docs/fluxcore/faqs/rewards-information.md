# Rewards Information

1. **When do I receive rewards?** As a FluxCore Provider, you can track your current rewards per hour in real-time. Rewards are credited when the rental is stopped by the customer or when you stop it due to a force stop or outage.
2. **Is there a minimum reward threshold before I receive rewards?** You receive rewards for a rental as soon as the rental is stopped. However, there is a minimum threshold implemented before you can withdraw them.
3. **Do I receive a weekly, monthly, or yearly reward statement either via CSV/PDF that I can use for tax purposes?** Yes, you can download a CSV statement from Account > Audit > Rewards Claimed. You may filter for specific types to get a cleaner download.
4. **Are there reward withdrawal fees?** A: Yes, there are reward withdrawal fees that depend on the network you withdraw them to.
5. **How are rewards paid out? For example, Crypto, PayPal, Stripe, etc.?** Provider rewards can either be transferred to FluxEdge Balance for direct use in machine rentals or withdrawn in the cryptocurrency FLUX on the ETH, Binance, and Mainnet networks.
6. **If I have reward questions or queries, where do I log a ticket to reach support?** Reward withdrawal requests are currently manually processed by the InFlux Technologies Team and can take up to 24 hours to process. If you have not received the rewards for a request, you can reach out to support at: [https://support.runonflux.io/support/home](https://support.runonflux.io/support/home).
7. **Can I set a minimum price for renting out my GPU?** Machine pricing is managed using a pricing formula, and currently, there are no specifications that the provider can set themselves.
