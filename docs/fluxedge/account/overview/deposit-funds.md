# Deposit Funds

#### **1. Accessing Your Account**

1. Visit the Website: Go to [marketplace.fluxedge.ai](https://marketplace.fluxedge.ai/) and sign in.
2. Navigate to Account: Use the left-hand menu to go to your account settings.
3. View Financial Overview: In the Overview section, you can see detailed information about your current financials:
   * Account Balance: Shows the current balance available for renting machines.
   * Current Spending: Displays your current hourly spending and the number of machines rented.

#### **2. Deposit Methods**

#### **A. PayPal**

1. Select PayPal: Click on the PayPal tile to access the deposit interface.
2. Enter Amount: Choose a predefined amount or enter a custom amount in the provided field.
3. Review Deposit Details:
   * Minimum deposit amount.
   * Applicable bonus during promotions.
   * Processing fee and the net credited amount.
4. Proceed to Payment:
   * Click "Pay Now" to go to the PayPal checkout page.
   * Log in to your PayPal account.
   * Select your payment method (PayPal account, linked card, or Pay Later option).
   * Review your order and complete the payment.
5. Confirmation: You will see a notification confirming the successful payment and updated account balance.

#### **B. Credit/Debit Cards**

1. Select Credit/Debit Card: Click on the Credit Card tile to enter the deposit interface.
2. Enter Amount: Choose a predefined amount or enter a custom amount.
3. Enter Card Details: Provide your card number, expiry date, and CVC.
   * Autofill and save card information options are available via Link.
4. Review Deposit Details:
   * Minimum deposit amount.
   * Applicable bonus during promotions.
   * Processing fee and the net credited amount.
5. Proceed to Payment: Click "Pay Now".
6. Confirmation: A notification will confirm the successful payment and updated account balance.

#### **C. Vouchers**

1. Select Vouchers: Click on the Voucher tile to enter the deposit interface.
2. Enter Voucher Code: Input your voucher code and click "Deposit Funds".
3. Confirmation: A notification will confirm the successful payment and updated account balance.

#### **D. FLUX**

1. Select FLUX: Click on the Flux logo tile to enter the deposit interface.
2. Network Selection: Choose the network for your FLUX deposit.
3. Copy Deposit Address: Use the copy button or QR code to get your deposit address.
   * Ensure to verify the address before use.
4. Review Deposit Details:
   * Current exchange rate of FLUX to fiat.
   * Minimum deposit amount.
   * Required blockchain confirmations.
   * Bonus deposit percentage for using FLUX.
5. Send FLUX: Send the chosen amount to the deposit address and wait for the blockchain confirmations.
6. Confirmation: A notification will confirm the successful payment and updated account balance.
