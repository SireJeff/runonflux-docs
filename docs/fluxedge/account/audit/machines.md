# Machines

The Audit Machines tab provides a complete history of machine rentals associated with your account.

You will see a data table with detailed information about all your rentals.

* Date: The date and time of the action.
* Machine: The machine rented, displaying the last 10 digits of its machine ID, along with uptime stats and reliability.
* Hardware: The machine's hardware, including GPU amount and model, as well as specs like cores, RAM, and storage.
* Location: The location of the machine, showing a masked IP address and its city and country.
* Price per Hour: The hourly rate paid for the rental.
* Duration: The duration of the rental.
* Total Spent: The total amount spent on this rental.
