# General Information

1. **What is FluxEdge?** FluxEdge is a service that customers access to a vast network of GPUs for machine learning, AI tasks, and other compute-intensive applications. By renting GPUs through FluxEdge, customers can save on hardware costs and benefit from scalable, on-demand compute resources.
2. **What types of GPUs are available?** FluxEdge offers a wide range of GPUs from various manufacturers, including NVIDIA, AMD, and others. We strive to provide a diverse selection of GPUs to meet the specific needs of our customers, whether they require high-performance GPUs for deep learning or more affordable options for general-purpose computing.
